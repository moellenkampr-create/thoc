## Style

```text
Band: THoC-V
Heavy Metal / True Metal with Punk-Rock edge, 130-134 BPM, driving 4/4, aggressive rhythm guitars, tight bass, hard-hitting drums, Badlands race atmosphere, raw studio energy.

Vocals are sung by three different people:
[Voice Lauri: young, raspy, gritty rock tone with emotional edge]
[Voice George: young, clear, powerful tenor with heroic presence]
[Voice Ely: young, bright, clean classical clarity as melodic anchor]

Arrangement:
- Verses alternate George and Lauri
- Ely mainly in chorus support lines
- Keep delivery direct, punchy, anthemic
- Minimal vocal FX, dry upfront leads
```
